Who we are

The New Lane Runners is a friendly club catering for runners, joggers and walkers of all abilities, from marathon runners to fair weather walkers. We have a very wide age range from early 20s to those in their 80s and have an equal male/female split with a membership of close to 150.

What we do

At meetings we split into 3 or so groups and run, walk or jog various distances and speeds. On Wednesdays we have a beginners group. On Saturday and Thursday our two meeting points give us access to the canal and an extensive network of footpaths leading to Safaricom Stadium and Kenyatta University among other places. On Wednesday evenings during the runner month, we do set routes around Bomasa, Langata, and Rongai. These vary between 7 and 8 miles but some groups take shorter routes based on them. We always make sure that everyone has someone to run with. Where and when we meet We meet three times a week:- Sundays at 9.15am, City Park, Parklands Avenue entrance. Wednesdays from January 2012 at 7.15pm, Nyayo Sports Club, 8 Park Avenue, Fridays at 7.00pm, Speed Training, Aga ghan sport club in Parkland. Until further notice, speed training is also being held on Saturdays at 9.15am, at Highrigde Park. Meet at Diamond plaza entrance. Wednesdays attract a larger number of people, with many stopping for a drink after their run.

Races

Although we are not principally a competitive club, many members enjoy participating in local races and we have a Cross Country team in a local Sunday League.

Social

There is a very active social side to the club, with at least one event a month organized. Events include meals, theatre trips, concerts and pub runs. We also try and get at least one trip abroad each year, for a long weekend incorporating a race somewhere.
